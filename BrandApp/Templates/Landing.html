Welcome to Brand Reputation Guard

Our app leverages the power of Facebook and Instagram APIs to provide you with valuable insights about your brand's reputation. Here's how it works:

Learn More
  • 1

    Data Gathering

    Our app integrates seamlessly with Facebook and Instagram APIs to fetch comments and engagements related to your brand. By connecting your brand's social media accounts, we gain access to a wealth of user-generated content.

  • 2

    Reputation Feedback

    Based on the sentiment analysis results, we generate a comprehensive reputation feedback report for your brand. This report provides an overview of the overall sentiment surrounding your brand, highlighting the percentage of positive, negative, and neutral comments received. By analyzing this feedback, you gain insights into how your brand is perceived by customers and identify areas that may require attention or improvement.

  • 3

    Visualization and Reporting

    Our app presents the reputation feedback report in an intuitive and visually appealing manner, making it easy for you to grasp the sentiment trends and patterns. You can view sentiment scores over time, compare different periods, and track the impact of your brand's initiatives or campaigns on sentiment. The reports can be customized and exported, allowing you to share the insights with your team or stakeholders.
